Abstract

This study outlines a multi-step synthesis of N-methyl-1-phenylpropan-2-amine from toluene using accessible lab reagents. The proposed sequence features benzylic chlorination, a Grignard reaction, ketonic decarboxylation, and reductive amination. Designed for educational use, this project demonstrates essential reaction mechanisms, safety considerations, and regulatory awareness under the NDPS Act.
